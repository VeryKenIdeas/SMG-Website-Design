Engine & Powertrain

Built to
Perform

The SMG Werks 993 Carrera RS Tribute Build is powered by a custom-built 3.6L engine equipped with RS camshafts, delivering performance characteristic of RS-spec engineering. Enhancing the auditory experience and maximizing exhaust flow is a performance X-pipe paired with tuned mufflers. The custom-built G50 close-ratio gearbox gives you sensational 2 to 3 shifts, while the RS lightweight flywheel and clutch kit compliments the rev-happy engine. To finish it off, the ECU has been custom-tuned, calibrated to RS specifications.

Suspension

Precision
Handling

Under the vehicle, it features a custom H&R coil-over suspension with adjustable camber plates, providing precise handling and ride-height flexibility. Upgraded front and rear adjustable sway bars, paired with RS tie-rods, ensure improved stability and cornering performance. Additionally, there is the 935 "X" strut brace enhancing overall structural integrity.

Braking & Wheels

Stopping
Power

This build is equipped with Porsche Factory Big Red calipers for exceptional stopping power, complemented by stainless-steel brake lines that enhance durability and braking precision. SMG-fitment Cup 2 wheels provide a perfect blend of lightweight performance and track-ready styling, secured with custom titanium lug nuts for strength and a sleek finishing touch.

Interior

Driver
Focused

The interior of this build showcases bespoke double-stitched red and black leather, beautifully accented with classic Pepita seat inserts for a timeless yet sporty look. Bespoke billet aluminum door handles and a billet sport short shifter lever add precision-crafted details, while a custom sport roll bar enhances safety and rigidity. Finishing touches include custom aluminum pedal boards, blending function with refined aesthetics to create a driver-focused cabin.
